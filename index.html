CUSTOM COMBAT

Refined PvP Mechanics

Our combat system is designed from the ground up to provide smooth, responsive, and balanced gameplay. Every kit has been carefully tuned to ensure fair matchups.

Key Features

  • Smooth hit registration with optimized networking
  • Balanced damage values across all weapons
  • Custom combo system with skill expression
  • No P2W advantages - skill-based gameplay only

Weapon Types

  • Swords - Versatile melee with balanced stats
  • Daggers - Fast attacks with lower damage
  • Axes - High damage with slower swing speed
  • Bows - Custom arrow mechanics with drop-off

HANDCRAFTED MAPS

Designed for Competition

Every map on Sekiya is custom-built by our team, designed specifically for intense PvP combat and strategic gameplay.

Map Philosophy

  • Balanced sightlines for ranged and melee combat
  • Multiple engagement zones and chokepoints
  • Vertical gameplay with height advantages
  • Cover options for tactical positioning

Current Maps

  • The Arena - Classic circular colosseum design
  • Ruins - Post-apocalyptic destroyed city
  • Skyfall - Floating islands with void pits

New maps are added regularly based on community feedback!

RANKED SYSTEM

Competitive Ladder

Test your skills against the best players on Sekiya with our ranked competitive system.

Rank Tiers

  • Bronze - Starting rank for all players
  • Silver - Developing skills
  • Gold - Solid fundamentals
  • Platinum - Advanced players
  • Diamond - Elite competitors
  • Champion - Top 100 players

Rewards

Climb the ranks to earn exclusive cosmetics, titles, and bragging rights. Season rewards are given at the end of each competitive season!

LIVE STATISTICS

Track Everything

Our comprehensive statistics system tracks every aspect of your gameplay so you can see how you stack up.

Tracked Stats

  • Kills, deaths, and K/D ratio
  • Win rate and games played
  • Kill streaks (current and best)
  • Damage dealt and received
  • Accuracy with ranged weapons
  • Time played and session data

Leaderboards

Compete for the top spots on our global leaderboards. Daily, weekly, and all-time boards available.

How to Obtain

  • Rank up in competitive for exclusive rewards

  • Participate in events for limited-time items
  • Support the server through our store
